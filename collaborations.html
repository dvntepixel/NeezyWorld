HOW IT STARTED

Built after a week in the paddock.

Early 2026, a few of us got a walk-through of a team garage during testing week — timing boards, radio checks, the exact stitch pattern on the pit crew's fireproof suits. We came back with a sketchbook full of details that had nothing to do with fashion and everything to do with precision.

This capsule is what happened when we tried to cut that precision into something you'd actually wear off the grid — heavier fabrics, tighter seams, a palette pulled straight off the carbon fiber. Same small-batch rules as always: numbered pieces, no reorders.
